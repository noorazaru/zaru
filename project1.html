STUDIO CAPSTONE PROJECT

Concept: combining Lenin Park and Alppila Park into a single green space The plan combines Lenin Park and Alpine Park into a single entity. The events and festivals that are part of Alppila’s identity and Linnanmäki are key sources of vitality for the area, and the new pink houses at the junction before Linnanmäki and the park underline the festival spirit of the area.

Sustainability and environmental responsibility guide the design: The church will not be demolished, as demolition is not suitable from a climate point of view.

Kolmas kuvaus jatkaa projektin tarinaa ja esittelee uusia näkökulmia.
