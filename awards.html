Helsinki: A Just City Blog Competition

Winner of the 2023 blog competition organized by Helsinki Institute of Urban and Regional Studies.

In the end of 2023, the Global Partnership Building project—a collaboration between University of Helsinki, Aalto University, and Newcastle University—launched a joint blog post competition on the topic of a just city. The award-winning blog post explored what makes a city just and equitable, addressing urban planning challenges and proposing innovative ideas for inclusive design.
