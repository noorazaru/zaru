About ZARU Studios

ZARU Studios is a Helsinki-based office redefining architecture as a practice that connects spaces and people, a platform to activate underused spaces.

Founded in 2024, ZARU Studios has a mission to transform overlooked places into communities of culture. Guided by this forward-thinking vision, we approach each project as a chance to reimagine what architecture can be: sustainable for the environment and socially.

This website showcases our philosophy and projects — a collection of projects that illustrate how architecture is problem-solving which can unite spaces and people in lasting and meaningful ways.

As of March 2024: Helsinki Metropolitan Area had approximately 616,000 m² of vacant office space, reflecting a vacancy rate of 14.5% (Rakli).

Before we build new buildings, we need to make use of these empty spaces. ZARU Studios' mission is to design spaces with property owners to solve this problem.

Our design philosophy centers on the belief that the future of architecture lies in rethinking how spaces serve the people who use them.

Has your space been vacant for a long time? Let us help you. Contact us at zarucontact@gmail.com.
